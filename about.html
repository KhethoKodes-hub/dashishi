Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in catering and supply services

Company History

Founded in 2017, Dashishi has grown from a small local supplier to a comprehensive service provider

2016

Our Beginnings

Started as a small meat supplier to local businesses and SANDF in Lohatla, focusing on quality chicken and red meat products.

2017

Official Registration

Dashishi Pty Ltd was formally registered (2017/113784/07) and expanded services to include catering for local events.

2019

Restaurant Opening

Opened our first restaurant in Postmasburg, expanding our catering capabilities and local presence.

2021

Service Expansion

Added cleaning supplies and printing services to our portfolio, becoming a one-stop solution provider.
